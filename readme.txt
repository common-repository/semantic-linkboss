== Description ==

**LinkBoss (Smartest Ever Interlinking Tool)** is the smartest ever interlinking tool designed to automate and optimize your internal linking strategy. Enhance your SEO efforts by creating meaningful and contextually relevant internal links effortlessly.

Supports manual incoming/outgoing, SILO, and bulk auto internal links.

**Features:**
* Automated internal linking based on semantic analysis.
* Customizable link settings and rules.
* Detailed link reports and analytics.
* Integration with popular SEO plugins.
* Easy-to-use interface with intuitive controls.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `semantic-linkboss` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the plugin settings from the 'LinkBoss' menu in the WordPress admin.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How does Semantic LinkBoss work? =
Semantic LinkBoss uses advanced algorithms to analyze your content and automatically create internal links that are contextually relevant and beneficial for SEO.

= Can I customize the linking rules? =
Yes, you can customize the linking rules and settings to match your specific needs and preferences.

= Is Semantic LinkBoss compatible with other SEO plugins? =
Yes, Semantic LinkBoss integrates seamlessly with popular SEO plugins to enhance your overall SEO strategy.
